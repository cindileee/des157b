Welcome to the Davis Centenniel Seal Treasure Hunt

How It Works:

  • Make sure to turn on location services in your Privacy settings of your phone for the best experience.
  • On the map page, click on the markers/badges to learn more about the differet seal locations
  • Switch to Badge Page to see all your badges and unlocked badges.
  • Use the routing function to go to a marker's location
  • To check in, take a photo at the location and upload it to the public gallery
